Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about CodeClash 2025

What programming languages are allowed?

▼

All popular programming languages including C, C++, Java, Python.

How do I register for the competition?

▼

Registration is done online via our portal. Click the "Register Now" button, fill in your details, and complete the payment process. You will receive a confirmation email upon successful registration.

What should I bring for the offline round?

▼

For the offline Grand Finale, participants should bring their college ID. All necessary hardware (laptops, monitors, etc.) and software setups will be provided.

When will results be announced?

▼

Results will be announced live at the end of the Grand Finale event on September 15, 2025. Official announcements will also be made on our social media channels.
